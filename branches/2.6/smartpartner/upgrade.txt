How to uprade from SmartPartner prior to 0.9
============================================

Unfortunatley, you will need to uninstall the module and reinstall it.


How to uprade from SmartPartner x.x
===================================

In order to upgrade the module, you will need to :

- Upload the complete /smartpartner folder into the /modules directory of your website.

- Then, log in to your site as administrator, go to System Admin > Modules, look for the SmartPartner icon in the list of installed modules and click in the update icon.

- Click on 'Update module' from the SmartPartner top menu, then press 'Ok'.

That's it !

Enjoy !
