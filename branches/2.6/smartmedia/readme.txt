What is SmartMedia ?
=================

SmartMedia is a XOOPS 2 module that let you manage a collection of multimedia clips, grouped by categories and folders. For the first time in XOOPS 2 history, this module offers an architecture supporting a real multilingual content management, managed by different forms !

To see what it looks like, visit http://demo.smartfactory.ca/modules/smartmedia/
